title: "Members"
---

The Members tab shows everyone who has joined your Kova Hub server.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Member Profiles" color="#3B82F6" icon="user">
    Access detailed member information including linked Discord and Roblox accounts, join dates, and activity history.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manage Roles" color="#8B5CF6" icon="shield">
    Assign or remove roles to control member permissions and organize your community hierarchy.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Remove Members" color="#EF4444" icon="xmark">
    Remove members from your server when needed to maintain community standards.
  </Card>

  <Card title="View Permissions" color="#F59E0B" icon="lock">
    See which members have "Manage Members" and "View Members" permissions at a glance.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Info>
  You need the **"Manage Members"** permission to edit members, and **"View Members"** to see the list.
</Info>
