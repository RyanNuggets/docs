title: "General Settings"
---

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Server Info" color="#3B82F6" icon="server">
    Update your server name, description, and icon at any time. This is the first impression members get of your community.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Join Codes" color="#10B981" icon="link">
    Generate invite codes that members can use to join your server. Set maximum use limits and revoke access instantly by deleting codes.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ten Codes" color="#F59E0B" icon="list">
    Define custom ten codes (e.g., 10-4, 10-8) that your units use during CAD sessions. These appear in the CAD interface for quick reference during operations.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Penal Codes" color="#EF4444" icon="scale">
    Create a comprehensive list of penal codes that officers can reference and attach to arrest records and citations for consistent enforcement.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Certifications" color="#8B5CF6" icon="badge">
    Assign certifications to members (e.g., FTO, K9, SWAT). Certifications are visible in CAD sessions to dispatch and other units for operational awareness.
  </Card>

  <Card title="CAD Settings" color="#06B6D4" icon="sliders">
    Configure global CAD behavior and preferences for your entire server. Control how your units interact with the CAD system.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Tip>
  Pro tip: Regularly review and update your ten codes and penal codes to ensure they reflect your department's current procedures and terminology. This keeps your team aligned and operations running smoothly.
</Tip>
