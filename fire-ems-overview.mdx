The Fire & EMS CAD gives Fire and EMS units their own interface separate from the Police MDT. It includes incident records, apparatus/roster management, and access to the live map.

## Access & Permissions

Users need the **"View Fire"** or **"View EMS"** permission to access their respective sections. Managing records or rosters requires the corresponding **Manage** permission.
