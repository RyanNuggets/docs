---
title: "Departments & Divisions"
---

Departments organize your community into distinct operational groups, such as Police, Fire, or DOT. Each department can contain multiple specialized divisions (K9 Unit, Detective Bureau, etc.). Members assigned to department roles see department-specific sections in the CAD.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the management panel">
    Navigate to **Manage Server** → **Departments & Divisions**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new department">
    Click **"Add Department"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the department">
    Enter a name and link it to a role
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm to create the department
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating Divisions

Divisions are created under an existing department and follow the same setup process as departments. Once created, they appear as sub-groups within their parent department.

<Tip>
  Link departments and divisions to roles for automatic permission management and streamlined member assignment.
</Tip>
