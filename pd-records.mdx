The Records section stores all formal documentation created during CAD sessions. Records are searchable and persist across sessions.

## Record Types

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Arrest Report" icon="file-lines">
    Full incident documentation including charges, narrative, and involved parties
  </Card>

  <Card title="Citation" icon="ticket">
    Traffic or civil infraction records

                                                   
  </Card>

  <Card title="Incident Report" icon="clipboard">
    General incident documentation not resulting in an arrest 
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Creating and Managing Records

Records can be created from within the CAD or attached to an active call.

**Required permissions:**

- **Create Records**: Write new records
- **Edit Records**: Update existing records
- **Delete Records**: Remove records
