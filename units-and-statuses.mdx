---
title: "Units & Statuses"
---

Every member signed into the CAD appears as a unit on the dispatch board. Units have a status that reflects their current availability.

Units can update their own status from within the CAD. Dispatchers with the **Manage Dispatch** permission can also update unit statuses directly.

**Common statuses:**

- **Available**: Ready to receive dispatch assignments
- **On Scene**: Currently at an incident location
- **Busy**: Engaged but not available for new assignments
- **Off Duty**: Not currently on dispatch duty

## Panic Button

Any unit can trigger a panic alert from their CAD. This immediately broadcasts an alert to all connected users on the server and plays an audio alert. The panic clears automatically after a set period or can be manually cleared.
