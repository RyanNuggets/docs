Kova Hub is a free, modern CAD (Computer Aided Dispatch) system built specifically for ER:LC (Emergency Response: Liberty County) communities on Roblox.

Whether you're running a small private server or a large community, Kova Hub gives your team a professional-grade dispatch board, police MDT, Fire/EMS CAD, DOT CAD, and full Discord integration — all at no cost.

## Core Features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Dispatch & Operations" icon="radio">
    Live dispatch board with real-time call management, unit tracking, and incident coordination.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Police MDT" icon="badge">
    Civilian lookups, warrants, BOLOs, and arrest records at your officers' fingertips.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Fire & EMS CAD" icon="fire">
    Incident records, rosters, and response management for emergency medical services.
  </Card>

  <Card title="DOT CAD" icon="truck">
    Citations, transport permits, and vehicle management for Department of Transportation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Discord Integration" icon="discord">
    Audit logs via bot integration and real-time notifications to your community.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Advanced Tools" icon="sliders">
    Role-based permissions, unit groups, certifications, ten codes, and more.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Additional Capabilities

- **Live in-game map and bodycam** via ERLC API integration
- **Customizable workflows** tailored to your community's needs
- **Scalable infrastructure** from small private servers to large communities
- **No cost** completely free to use and deploy
