---

Kova Hub supports two account types to enhance your experience:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Discord" color="#5865F2" icon="discord">
    **Required**: Sign in to Kova Hub using Discord OAuth. This verifies your identity across servers and allows the audit log bot to recognize you.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Roblox" color="#00A2E8" icon="gamepad-modern">
    **Optional**: Link your Roblox account to display your username and avatar alongside your CAD sessions. Servers may require you to link your account.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Note>
  Kova Hub uses account connections to verify your identity and personalize your experience across servers.
</Note>

## Linking Your Roblox Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    Navigate to your Kova Hub dasbhoard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Roblox Connection">
    Locate the Roblox account connection option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the Connection Flow">
    Complete the Roblox OAuth authorization process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Profile">
    Your Roblox username and avatar will now appear in your CAD sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Unlinking Your Roblox Account

To disconnect your Roblox account from Kova Hub:

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Find the **Roblox Connection** section
3. Click **Unlink**

Your Roblox data will be removed from your profile immediately.

<Tip>
  Unlinking your Roblox account does not affect your Discord connection or any of your Kova Hub data.
</Tip>
