---
title: "Creating a Server"
---

After logging in with Discord, you'll land on your dashboard, your central hub for managing all Kova Hub operations. From here, you can create and configure your first server in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Server'">
    From your dashboard, locate and click the "Create Server" button in the top right or main navigation area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basic Settings">
    Enter your server name and a brief description. The name should reflect your organization or community (e.g., "Los Santos Police Department").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a Server Icon (Optional)">
    Add a custom icon to make your server instantly recognizable. Recommended size: 512×512 pixels. Supported formats: PNG, JPG.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Access Management">
    Click "Create" to finalize your server. You'll be automatically redirected to your new server's management dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You are automatically the owner of any server you create. Ownership cannot be transferred to another user.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Start with basic configuration and gradually add advanced features as your team grows. Most organizations benefit from setting up Discord integration and ERLC API connection first, as these unlock core functionality.
</Tip>
