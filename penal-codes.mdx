Penal codes are configured by server admins in the General settings and are available in the CAD for officers to reference and attach to records.

## Using Penal Codes

When writing an arrest report or citation, officers can select applicable penal codes directly from the list rather than typing them manually. This keeps records **consistent across your community**.

## Managing Penal Codes

To manage penal codes, go to **Manage Server → General → Penal Codes**.
