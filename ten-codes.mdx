---
title: "Ten Codes"
---

Ten codes are short radio codes your community uses during operations to keep communication consistent and efficient. Examples include **10-4** for acknowledged and **10-8** for in service.

Ten codes are visible in the CAD as a quick reference for all active units. They serve as a reference tool only — they don't have a mechanical function in the system.

## Managing Ten Codes

Server admins configure the ten codes list in your server settings.

**To manage ten codes:**

1. Go to **Manage Server**
2. Select **General**
3. Navigate to **Ten Codes**

From there, you can add, edit, or remove codes as needed for your community's operations.
