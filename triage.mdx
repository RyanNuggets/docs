The Triage tab in the Fire/EMS CAD is a Mass Casualty Incident (MCI) tracking tool. It gives your team a live board to log, categorise, and monitor patients during large-scale emergencies.

### Adding patients

For each patient, fill in the following fields:

- **Name**: Patient identifier (e.g. "Pt. 1" or an actual name)
- **Chief Complaint**: The presenting problem (e.g. "chest pain, unconscious")
- **Priority**: The triage category (see categories below)
- **Assigned Unit**: Optionally assign a unit to that patient

### Triage board

All added patients appear on the triage board. A live counter at the top tracks the total number of patients per category, giving you an at-a-glance overview of the scene's severity. Each patient card shows a running timer since they were added.

### Clearing the triage

Click **Clear Patients** to reset the board once the MCI event is resolved.
