## Creating a Call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Call' on the dispatch board">
    Open the call creation interface from the main dispatch board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in call details">
    Enter the call title, type, and any additional notes relevant to the incident.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign units">
    Select and assign the appropriate units to respond to the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit the call — it appears on the board immediately and is ready for dispatch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing a Call

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="View & Edit" icon="pencil">
    Click any call to open its details panel. From here, you can assign or unassign units, update the call status, and add notes as the situation develops.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Close the Call" icon="check">
    Once the incident is resolved, close the call to remove it from active dispatch and archive it for records.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## 911 Calls from In-Game

<Info>
  If your ERLC webhook is configured, calls placed in-game appear automatically on the dispatch board. A chime plays when a new 911 call comes in, alerting dispatchers immediately. Handle in-game calls the same way as manual calls — assign units, update status, and close when resolved.
</Info>
