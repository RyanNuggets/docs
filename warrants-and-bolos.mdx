## Warrants

Officers can issue active warrants linked to a civilian name. Warrants appear in search results when that civilian is looked up, alerting other officers to their status.

**Key features:**

- Active warrants display in civilian search results
- Warrants can be marked as served
- Warrants can be removed from the system

## BOLOs (Be On the Lookout)

BOLOs are broadcast notices for vehicles or individuals of interest. All active units can see current BOLOs in the CAD in real time.

**BOLO details include:**

- Description of the vehicle or individual
- Reason for the BOLO
- Visibility to all active units
