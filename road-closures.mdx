title: "Road Closures"
---

The Roads tab gives DOT units a live board to log and track active road closures, detours, and hazards across the map.

## Adding a closure

Click **\+ Add Closure** and fill in the following fields:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Road / Location" icon="map-pin">
    The street name or intersection (e.g., "Main St & 4th Ave"). Pin the location directly on the live map using the Map button.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Closure Type" icon="alert-circle">
    Choose from: Full Closure, Partial / Lane, Detour Active, Construction Zone, or Hazard / Spill.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Detour / Notes" icon="clipboard">
    Add detour instructions or additional context (e.g., "Use Oak St via 3rd").
  </Card>

  <Card title="Assigned Unit" icon="users">
    Optionally assign a unit to manage the closure.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Est. Duration" icon="clock">
    Set the estimated time the closure will be in effect.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Managing closures

Active closures appear in the list on the left side of the screen. Use these actions to maintain your closures:

- **Edit**: Click any closure to modify its details
- **Clear**: Remove a closure once it's resolved
- **Clear All**: Remove all active closures at once
