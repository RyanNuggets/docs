---
title: "Discord Integration"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Discord Integration Settings">
    Navigate to **Manage Server** → **Integrations** → **Discord Audit Log**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the Bot">
    Click **Add to Discord** to invite the bot to your server. You'll be redirected automatically after authorizing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Log Channel">
    Select the Discord channel where you want activity logs sent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Events to Log">
    Enable or disable individual event types based on your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Configuration">
    Click **Save** to apply your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Loggable Events

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Session Events" icon="clock">
    - CAD session started
    - CAD session ended
    - CAD session time
  </Card>

  <Card title="Call Management" icon="phone">
    - Calls created
    - Calls updated
    - Calls closed
  </Card>

  <Card title="Unit Operations" icon="alert-circle">
    - Unit panic triggered
  </Card>

  <Card title="Team Changes" icon="users">
    - Role changes
    - Member joins/removals
    - Department updates
  </Card>

  <Card title="Security" icon="key">
    - PRC key changed
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Tip>
  You can re-add the bot at any time if it's removed from your Discord. Logs will resume automatically once it's reconnected.
</Tip>
