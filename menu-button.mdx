---
title: "Menu Button"
---

The menu button in the top-right corner of the CAD opens a dropdown with quick access to tools, floating windows, and settings. Most items open as floating, draggable windows that you can reposition and resize anywhere on screen — so they don't take you away from whatever view you're working in.

<Tip>
  Every floating window has controls in its title bar — minimize (collapse to just the title bar), maximize (expand to fill more space), lock (pin it in place so it can't be accidentally dragged), and close.
</Tip>

## Session & Navigation

<Card title="Leave CAD" icon="sign-out-alt">

</Card>

<Card title="Switch Panel" icon="exchange-alt">

## Preferences

<Card title="Preferences" icon="sliders-h">
  Opens the preferences window where you can configure your personal CAD settings. Changes are saved to your browser and persist between sessions.

  **Audio Settings**

  - Master Volume — Controls the overall volume of all CAD audio
  - TTS Volume — Controls the volume of text-to-speech announcements
  - TTS Voice — Choose from available system voices for TTS readouts
  - Call Chime Notifications — Toggles the chime for incoming calls
  - On-Call Sound — Toggles the speaker sound when dispatched
  - Mute All Sounds — Silences all CAD audio at once
  - No Sound When Busy / Off Duty — Suppresses call sounds when status is Busy or Off Duty
  - Radio Volume — Controls the volume of incoming radio transmissions
  - Radio Click Sound — Toggles the click sound when a unit keys up

  **Device Settings**

  - Input Device (Mic) — Select which microphone to use for radio transmissions
  - Output Device (Speaker) — Select which audio output device to use for radio audio

  **Map Settings**

  - Map Style — Choose between standard map (No Postals) or postal code overlay (With Postals)

  A "Stop All Sounds" button is also available to immediately cancel any playing audio or TTS.
</Card>

<Card title="Enter Full Screen" icon="expand">
  Puts the CAD into browser full screen mode for a distraction-free experience.
</Card>

## Floating Windows

<Card title="Notepad" icon="sticky-note">
  Opens a floating notepad window for taking freeform notes during your session. Create multiple tabs within the notepad to keep different notes separate (e.g., one tab per active call). Tabs can be added, renamed, and deleted. Notes are stored locally in your browser.
</Card>

<Card title="Radio" icon="microphone">
  Opens the floating radio window to manage your radio channel and transmit to other units.

  - **Channel selection** — Choose which channel (CH 1, CH 2, etc.) you're monitoring and transmitting on
  - **PTT (Push to Talk)** — Click and hold the PTT button to transmit, or press and hold Spacebar
  - **Units list** — Shows other units currently keyed up on your channel with a speaking indicator
</Card>

<Card title="Certifications" icon="certificate">
  Opens a floating window showing your currently active certifications for this CAD session, and lets you update which certifications are active.
</Card>

<Card title="Unit Timer" icon="hourglass-end">
  Opens a floating countdown timer window. Useful for tracking time-sensitive situations like traffic stops or scene timers.

  - Set a label (e.g., "Traffic Stop", "Scene Timer")
  - Set the countdown duration in minutes and seconds
  - Live countdown display with TTS readout when it expires
  - TTS button to have the current elapsed time read out at any point
</Card>

<Card title="Groups" icon="users">
  Opens the floating unit groups window, where you can create, join, and manage unit groups directly without navigating away from your current view. See Unit Groups for full details on how groups work.
</Card>
