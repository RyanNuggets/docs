Fire and EMS records capture incident documentation for emergency responses.

## Creating a Record

From the Fire or EMS CAD, click **New Incident** and fill in the details:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic Information" icon="file-alt">
    - **Incident type** — Classification of the emergency
    - **Location** — Address or coordinates of the incident
  </Card>

  <Card title="Response Details" icon="users">
    - **Units involved** — Responding fire/EMS units
    - **Narrative** — Detailed account of the incident
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Card title="Patient Information" icon="heart-pulse">
  Medical details (EMS records only)
</Card>

## Managing Records

Records are stored and searchable throughout the system. Staff with the appropriate **manage** permission can edit or delete records.
